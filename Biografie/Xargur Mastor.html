Biography of Xargur Mastor Kadekeif

Xargur Mastor Kadekeif was born on June 2nd 422 p.I. in a wealthy family in Kadekei. His mother was Fayo Mumurbesto Kadekeif, and she was a neurosurgeon. His father instead was Maxastur Robombesto Kadekeif, and he was a philosophy professor at Kadekei Univeristy.

His parents had never been very present for him because of their jobs, so he grew up with his aunt, Laryo Flugorbesto Kadekeif. She was a really strict woman who always wanted him to be a model student and to have the best grades. To go in contrast with Laryo's requests, he always did the bare minimum, something for which she always went mad and argued.

At the age of 6 Xargur enrolled in Kadekei's "Pukyo" elementary school, where he became good friend with Kafkampo Borbesto Kadekeif and Mayur Fixurbestor Borvozhkeif. His teachers described him as a quarrelsome, smart, and very curious person. He never had very good grades, but at the same time he never had any big problem. In his class, apart from his two friends, he had troubles interacting with his classmates. During recess he either spent his time alone or with Kafkampo or Mayur.

At the age of 16 he moved on to high school. In this period he fell in love with 18-year-old Navyaskur Shaombestor Kadekeif after meeting her at a concert. Because of her, Xargur started to drink alcohol and became addicted. Due to this, he got expelled from his high school. He was so ashamed for this that he didn't want to meet his parents again to give them this piece of news. So at the age of 17 he went to live at Navyaskur's house.

Two months later, Navyaskur passed away due to alcohol intoxication. After discovering this, Xargur decided to change his life and get clean, so he got help and stopped drinking. He enrolled in the military the day of his 19th birthday, and became a field doctor. He immediately got into action because of some anti-terrorism operations.

In December of 401 p.I. he was shot in his left hand, of which he lost the ability to movee, so he left the army to join a private kondo militia in the logistic team.

In March of 400 p.I. he started to take part in many riots for kondo indipendence. In May, he took part in the Revolt of May. After the massacre, he couldn't take it any more, so he escaped society to plan a big attack. He and the Cave Companions hid themselves in a big cave inside Xargur Mountains, where they planned what they originally called "The Big One".

Later, during the First Civil War, Xargur remained hidden in a bunker in the country of Tehera with Qi and Somoa, from where they gave orders to their troops. At the end of the war, he became the representative figure of Aspakei in international contextes. In October of 397 he presented himself for the first time at the International Congress, but he didn't immediately convince everyone.

In 395 p.I. he married Luvinkampur Borbesto Isozhkeif, an economist and senator. Together they moved to Isozhkeif and had three children, a boy, Pashtor Mumurbesto Isozhkeif and two girls, Nevor Temporbesto and Xalor Flugorbesto Isozhkeif.

In 388 p.I. he went back to school and in 384 he took his high school degree. He then went to HLS to study law.

September 18th, 375 p.I. at the age of 47, he was shot to death during an international meeting by Elu-extremist Gou Tam Ku.
